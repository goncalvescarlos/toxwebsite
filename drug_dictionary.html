Drug Dictionary

Lay-language explanations of substances detected in toxicology testing.

Acetaminophen

Class: Analgesic

Common pain reliever and fever reducer. High doses (over ~10g total) can cause serious liver damage.

Fentanyl

Class: Opioid

Extremely potent opioid (80–100× stronger than morphine). Overdose can rapidly cause breathing failure.

Alprazolam

Class: Benzodiazepine

Used for anxiety and panic disorders. High doses can cause sedation, coma, and withdrawal seizures.
